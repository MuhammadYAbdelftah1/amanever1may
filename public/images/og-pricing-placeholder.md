# OG Image for Pricing Page

## Required Image: `og-pricing.jpg`

### Specifications
- **Dimensions**: 1200 x 630 pixels
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File size**: < 1MB (recommended)

### Content Requirements
The image should include:

1. **أمان إيفر Logo** (top center or top right)
2. **Main Headline**: "ابتداءً من 199 ريال/شهر"
3. **Three Pricing Tiers** displayed as cards:
   - أمان: 199 ريال
   - أمان بلس: 349 ريال (highlighted)
   - أمان VIP: 699 ريال
4. **Key Benefit**: "خصومات تصل 80%"
5. **Trust Element**: "500+ منشأة طبية"

### Design Guidelines
- Use brand colors: #4A8B8E (teal), #E8F1F1 (light teal)
- RTL layout (Arabic text flows right to left)
- Clean, professional design
- High contrast for social media visibility
- Include subtle gradient background

### Tools to Create
- Canva (recommended for quick creation)
- Figma
- Adobe Photoshop
- Any graphic design tool
